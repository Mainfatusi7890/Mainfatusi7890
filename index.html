UNIMED RANKED 19TH BEST UNIVERSITY IN NIGERIA, 3RD BEST
SPECIALISED UNIVERSITY

The University of Medical Sciences (UNIMED), Ondo City, Ondo State has
been ranked by the National Universities Commission (NUC) as the 19th
best University in Nigeria.
The latest ranking, released on Monday, December 13, 2021, was carried
out using twelve indicators to rate one hundred and thirteen (113) federal,
state and private universities across the country. (Some universities did not
participate in the ranking exercise)
